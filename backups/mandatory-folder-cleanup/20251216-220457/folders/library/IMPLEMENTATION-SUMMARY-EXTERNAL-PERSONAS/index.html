    üéØ Key Achievements¶

    1. Conceptual Clarity¶

    SPxx (Service Personas) = What users see - External-facing EVA services - Examples: Senior Advisor, Data Ingestion, ATO Auditor - Governed per GC Agentic AI Framework (C0-C3 autonomy) - Require PIAs, AIIAs, risk registers

    Pxx (DevTools Patterns) = How we build - Internal development patterns - Examples: Requirements Engine (P02), Scaffolder (P05), Swarm Review (P17) - Governed per internal SDLC standards - Documented with pattern specs, runbooks, workflows

    2. Autonomy Levels Mapped¶

    All patterns and personas labeled with C0-C3 autonomy levels per GC Agentic AI Framework:

    • C0 (Human-in-the-loop): AI suggests, human approves every action
    • C1 (Human-on-the-loop): AI acts autonomously, human monitors
    • C2 (Human-over-the-loop): AI acts with constraints, human reviews periodically
    • C3 (Full autonomy): AI acts fully autonomously (not yet implemented)

    3. Complete Cross-References¶

    All patterns/personas link to: - Related patterns (dependencies, integrations) - Related personas (service interactions) - Related repos (implementation locations) - Related frameworks (GC Agentic AI Framework, TBS policies, SSC controls)
