    3 Autonomy Levels¶

    1. Advisory       ‚Üí Retrieves/explains only (NO recommendations)
2. Assistive      ‚Üí Proposes + drafts (REQUIRES human review)
3. Semi-Autonomous ‚Üí Low-risk tasks (NO final decisions on rights/benefits)

    3 Lifecycle Tiers¶

    1. Sandbox    ‚Üí Experimental, low-risk data, restricted
2. Pilot      ‚Üí Limited audience, documented governance, monitored
3. Production ‚Üí Full integration, complete governance evidence

    üìã Compliance Checklist¶

    Must Have [CORE]¶

    • Human-in-the-loop design
    • Governed & auditable behavior
    • WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility (or 2.2 AAA for components)
    • Bilingual support (EN/FR)
    • Kill switch capability
    • Logging and traceability
    • Project space boundaries
    • RBAC enforcement

    Security & Privacy¶

    • Operates within approved classifications
    • Data residency compliant
    • Encryption at rest & in transit
    • Versioned model tracking

    Observability¶

    • Structured logs
    • Correlation IDs across requests
    • Configuration change tracking
    • Exportable reports for oversight

    üí° Quick Answers¶

    Q: What is the GC agentic framework?
    A: A 5-volume, technology-neutral template system (~2,600 lines) for federal departments to design, build, and govern AI agents safely.

    Q: Where are the templates?
    A: /archive/2025-11-25-intake/to-upload/gc-agentic-ai-template/

    Q: What's the difference between [CORE] and [ADV]?
    A: [CORE] = foundational minimum, [ADV] = advanced/future capabilities

    Q: How many autonomy levels?
    A: 3 - Advisory, Assistive, Semi-Autonomous

    Q: How many lifecycle tiers?
    A: 3 - Sandbox, Pilot, Production

    Q: Is EVA aligned with GC templates?
    A: Yes, extensively. EVA Crew Charter explicitly aligns with GC agentic templates (Volumes 0-4)

    Q: Can I use this for other departments?
    A: Yes! That's the purpose - technology-neutral, reusable across GC

    Q: What about accessibility?
    A: WCAG 2.1 AA minimum (Volume 1), WCAG 2.2 AAA for GC components

    Q: Is this production-ready?
    A: Yes, proven through EVA 2.0 implementation (15 repos bootstrapped)
